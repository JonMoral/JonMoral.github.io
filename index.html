JONATHAN MORALES-ROCHA

ABOUT ME

My name is Jonathan Morales-Rocha, I am a Game Developer based in Santa Cruz, CA. I am a father of an incredibly smart and shy toddler that never ceases to amaze me on a daily basis.

I have loved games since I could remember, it wasn't until I was 10 that I found that making games was something you could actually do for a living. Since then, I have strived to create games, although I never tried making anything until I was 18 (a terrrible Blender Game Engine FPS).

It took me years to gather the courage to be okay with failing, and a little bit longer to appreciate those failures and use them as learning opportunities.

Today, I love working hard to understand something and failure in doing so is only a stepping stone for all the learning that is happening.

Today, I am happy to fail because tomorrow I'll be a better Game Developer for it.

This is me bro.
