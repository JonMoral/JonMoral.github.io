JUANITO EL NAHUALITO

Juanito el Nahualito is a short narrative adventure that follows the story of a modern Latin American teen who re-discoveres his ability to spiritually connect with nature while searching for his missing grandfather.

Throughout the adventure, he discovers that his abilities are inherited by his mesoamerican ancestors, the Nahuales.

The game uses 2D pre-rendered cinematics and systems in a 3D space to provide historical information about mesoamerican culture and Nahuales.
