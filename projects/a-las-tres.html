A Las Tres

A Las Tres is a narrative game about immigration.

A father has traveled from México to the U.S. in order to find better opportunities to sustain his family back home. The father works as a taquero every day except Sunday trying to make ends meet and make sure his family has all they want.

He spends his Sundays at 3:00PM telling his young daughter the story of his migration and why he can't be with her.
